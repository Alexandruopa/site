Corporate Identity

TECVIVO S.R.L. is an established property management and leasing enterprise headquartered in Brasov, Romania. Operating within the core Real Estate sector, our firm specializes in the acquisition, management, and leasing of premium real property assets.

As authorized Lessors of Real Estate, we bridge the gap between architectural infrastructure and the people who inhabit it. We cater to both residential tenants seeking modern living spaces and commercial enterprises requiring strategic business locations in the heart of Brasov.

Tecvivo Resident Portal

To provide our tenants with a seamless, modern leasing experience, TECVIVO S.R.L. develops and maintains a proprietary mobile application published on the Google Play Store.

The Tecvivo Resident app transforms how you interact with your leased property. It serves as a digital concierge, allowing tenants to pay rent securely, request maintenance, and unlock smart doors directly from their smartphones.

  • Secure mobile rent payments and digital lease invoicing.
  • Instant maintenance requests with photo uploads and status tracking.
  • Digital smart-key integration for contactless building entry.
  • Direct messaging channel to the property management team.
